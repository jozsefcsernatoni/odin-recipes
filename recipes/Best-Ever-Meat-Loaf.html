Best Ever Meat Loaf

Home

Description

This meat loaf recipe features baked-in Cheddar cheese for rich flavor and a gooey texture. Home cooks say the savory-sweet glaze creates a juicy loaf that wins over even picky eaters. Allrecipes member Donna claims, "This was absolutely the best meatloaf I have ever made."

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slices bread, crumbled
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded carrot
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Add crumbled bread and stir until dissolved. Mix ground beef, onion, Cheddar cheese, and carrot into bread mixture; transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in a small bowl; spread over the meat mixture.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 60 to 75 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
